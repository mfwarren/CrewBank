So what's this all about?

CrewBank is a social community built to support yacht racing. It allows skippers to find and communicate with crew for race days in a way that allows them to manage their team, find replacements at short notice, and also helps to give new sailors a chance to experience sailing and learn about this wonderful sport.

The goal of CrewBank is to give sailors the tools for robust and accountable communication needed to build friendships

We believe that getting more people the opportunity to get out on the water more often is the best way to drive increased engagement, grow memberships and foster a stronger community for your sailing club or marina
